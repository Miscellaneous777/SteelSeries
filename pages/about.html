About Us

Steelseries was formed with one idea: Innovation. Since we formed in 2001, our company was formed to meet the surface needs of hardcore gamers. SteelSeries is dedicated to building products that push professional gaming further than anyone dreamed. Those innovations make gaming more competitive and more fun for gamers everywhere. Our fans, customers, and professional esports teams demand the ultimate performance from SteelSeries. We combine the necessity of professional-grade quality and durability with our unyielding pursuit of simple, modern design.
